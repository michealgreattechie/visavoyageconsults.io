What is a Visiting Visa?

A visiting visa is designed for those travelling to see family or friends abroad — not for tourism or employment. It requires strong ties to your home country and clear evidence of an invitation. We build your file to satisfy exactly what embassies look for.

Who Is It For?

  • Visiting parents, children, or siblings abroad
  • Attending a wedding, graduation, or family event
  • Short-stay guests of residents or citizens
  • Medical care with a family host in destination country

Profile Assessment & Shortlisting

We start with a detailed profile assessment reviewing:

  • Your grades, test scores, and academic background
  • Your budget and financial situation
  • Your career goals and field of interest
  • Your preferred country or destination

We then match you to programs and universities where you have a realistic chance of both admission and visa approval — protecting your money and your time.

This is one of the most important things to understand before applying — and we explain it clearly during your assessment so you don't waste money on the wrong applications.

  • Universities offer degree-level programs (Bachelor's, Master's, PhD) and typically have more competitive entry requirements
  • Colleges offer diplomas, certificates, and vocational training — often more flexible entry requirements and faster to complete
  • Pathway programs help students who don't meet direct entry requirements bridge into a degree program — often after 1 semester

We offer a free initial consultation to assess your profile and discuss your options with no obligation. Our service fees depend on the scope of support you need — from application-only packages to full end-to-end support including visa processing.

We are transparent about our pricing. Reach out to us and we'll walk you through exactly what's included before you commit to anything.
Course & Admission Application

We handle the full application process so you don't have to figure it out alone:

  • Selecting the right course based on employability — not just rankings
  • Filling in application forms and uploading all required documents
  • Drafting Letters of Explanation (LOEs) and tracking deadlines
  • Reviewing and editing your Statement of Purpose (SOP) and CV
  • Helping you secure strong Letters of Recommendation (LORs)
  • Applying to multiple universities simultaneously to maximise your odds

Timelines vary by country, institution, and intake period. Generally:

  • Document preparation and profile assessment: 1–2 weeks
  • Application submission to offer letter: 2–8 weeks depending on the institution
  • Visa processing: 4–12 weeks depending on the country
We advise starting at least 6 months before your intended intake date to avoid rushed applications and missed deadlines.

Requirements vary by country, institution, and program level. During your profile assessment, we review exactly where you stand and only recommend schools where you have a genuine shot — so you're not wasting application fees on long shots.

  • If you don't meet direct entry requirements, we may recommend a pathway or foundation program
  • Language proficiency (IELTS, TOEFL, Duolingo) requirements differ by country and school
  • We explain all requirements upfront — no surprises
Visa Processing Support

We prepare and manage your entire visa file to meet embassy requirements for Canada, UK, Ireland, France, China, USA, and Australia. This includes:

  • Building a complete, embassy-ready visa file
  • Explaining financial, health, and language requirements per country
  • Mock interview preparation to reduce refusal risk
  • Document checks before submission
  • Submitting and tracking your application through to a decision

Our goal is to minimise refusal risk from the start through thorough document checks and mock interview preparation. However, if a visa is refused, we:

  • Review the refusal reason with you in detail
  • Advise on whether a re-application or appeal is appropriate
  • Help strengthen the file for a second attempt where possible
Prevention is better than cure — that's why we're thorough before we submit, not after.

Processing times vary by country and season:

  • 🇨🇦 Canada: 8–12 weeks on average
  • 🇬🇧 UK: 3–6 weeks
  • 🇮🇪 Ireland: 4–8 weeks
  • 🇦🇺 Australia: 4–8 weeks
  • 🇺🇸 USA: varies widely — interview appointment availability is a key factor
  • 🇫🇷 France & 🇨🇳 China: 4–8 weeks depending on intake period
We always advise applying early. We track your application and keep you updated throughout the process.

  • Cultural orientation tips so you're not caught off guard
  • Accommodation booking assistance in select cases
